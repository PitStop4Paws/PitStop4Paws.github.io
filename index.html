What We Do

We believe every pet should feel safe, warm, and cared for — no matter where they call home. Pit Stop 4 Paws provides practical, hands-on support to keep pets of the houseless community healthy and comfortable through every season.

Warmth & Protection

Coats, booties, and rain covers to keep pets dry and comfortable in Oregon weather.

Everyday Essentials

Leashes, harnesses, and flea/tick prevention — practical gear for daily life on the move.

Care & Referrals

Connections to low-cost veterinary care and trusted community resources for both pets and their people.
