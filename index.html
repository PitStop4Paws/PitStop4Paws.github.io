What We Do

We believe pets of all walks of life should feel safe, warm, and cared for on the hardest days. Pit Stop 4 Paws brings warmth, safety, and joy to the pets of the houseless community — one pit stop at a time.

Warmth & Protection

Coats, booties, and rain covers to keep pets dry and comfortable in Oregon weather.

Everyday Essentials

Leashes, harnesses, bowls, and flea/tick prevention — practical gear for daily life on the move.

Care & Referrals

Connections to low-cost vet care and community resources for both pets and people.
